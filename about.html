Our Story

From a Small Press to a National Leader

AXE Packages was founded in 2008 by Mr. SHEIKH ZUBAIR. The company grew rapidly by focusing on one principle: never compromise on quality.

Today, AXE Packages operates production facilities across Lahore, delivering millions of packaging units annually to satisfied clients nationwide.

Our journey is a testament to Pakistan's manufacturing potential — proof that world-class packaging can be made right here at home.
